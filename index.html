tzedeka trading (pty) ltd is 100% black owned a civil, mechanical engineering and construction supply company with excellent capabilities over a wide range of different technical and maintenance fields in the civil and mechanical engineering industry since 2016. It is registered with the construction industry development board (cidb) as a work within the scope of grade: 6CE, 3GB, 3ME and 1EP.

OUR MISSION
To develop, build, service and supply Southern Africa's requirement for a better living, working and travelling conditions in order to improve the quality of life for all. The company is dedicated to maintaining professional standards within the civil and mechanical engineering industry. tzedeka trading is committed to putting its clients first and to promote long-term relationships within the private and public sectors.
OUR STRATEGY
tzedeka trading objective is to deliver optimal cost-effective solutions that fulfill the client's engineering, local authority's specifications and requirements expeditiously with complete legitimacy. Our success greatly depends on our proven capability to adapt to the unique, changing and challenging developmental needs of Southern Africa.
